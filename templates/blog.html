Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis App

Using Flask & Machine Learning to Empower Healthcare

The Problem

With overlapping symptoms and lack of access to medical help, users often struggle to understand their health conditions. We aimed to build a digital tool that bridges this gap and gives users instant insight.

The Solution

  1. Symptom Input: Easy-to-use form for entering symptoms.
  2. Model Prediction: A Decision Tree model trained on symptom-disease data.
  3. Instant Diagnosis: Fast and accurate output based on symptoms.
  4. Info Rich: Disease overview, precautions, medication, diet, and workouts.

Key Features

User-Friendly UI

Simple and intuitive experience for all age groups.

ML-Powered

Accurate predictions using trained decision tree model.

Educational

Informative blog and health resources integrated in app.

Responsive

Fully responsive across mobile, tablet and desktop.

Conclusion

Our diagnosis app blends healthcare and machine learning to offer a fast, accurate, and informative solution. Whether at home or on the go, users are empowered with insights that can guide their next health steps.
